# рҹ”— LinkedIn Unicode Bold Heading Guide

LinkedIn does **not** support Markdown (`**bold**`, `# Heading`).  
To get **bold headings**, use **Unicode Mathematical Bold** characters вҖ” they render as bold in LinkedIn posts.

---

## рҹ“Ӣ Quick Copy-Paste: Bold Alphabet

```

### lowercase Bold
```
рқ—® рқ—Ҝ рқ—° рқ—ұ рқ—І рқ—і рқ—ҙ рқ—ө рқ—¶ рқ—· рқ—ё рқ—№ рқ—ә рқ—» рқ—ј рқ—Ҫ рқ—ҫ рқ—ҝ рқҳҖ рқҳҒ рқҳӮ рқҳғ рқҳ„ рқҳ… рқҳҶ рқҳҮ

## рҹ’Ў Pro Tips for LinkedIn Posts

1. **Hook first** вҖ” Your first line is all that shows before "see more". Make it count.
2. **One idea per post** вҖ” Don't try to say everything. Pick one insight.
3. **Short paragraphs** вҖ” Max 2вҖ“3 lines per block. White space = readability.
4. **End with a question** вҖ” Drives comments which boosts the algorithm.
5. **Post at peak times** вҖ” TueвҖ“Thu, 8вҖ“10 AM or 12вҖ“1 PM your local timezone.
6. **No external links in body** вҖ” Put links in the first comment to avoid reach penalty.
